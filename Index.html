About Us

Seed Tech Innovations: Leading the Future of Surveying with DGPS & Drone Technology Seed Tech Innovations stands at the forefront of modern surveying, pioneering the use of DGPS (Differential Global Positioning System) and UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) technologies to deliver cutting-edge geospatial solutions. Our commitment to innovation and precision positions us as a trusted partner for a wide range of industries seeking accurate, efficient, and cost-effective survey data. Equipped with state-of-the-art drones and high-accuracy DGPS systems, we specialize in delivering: High-resolution topographic mapping for land development and infrastructure planning Detailed contour surveys to support elevation profiling and design Precise volume analysis essential for mining, stockpile management, and excavation tracking Advanced planning inputs for smart agriculture, road networks, urban layouts, and construction workflows Our solutions are tailored for critical sectors including infrastructure development, mining operations, agriculture modernization, and construction management. By leveraging drone-based photogrammetry and ground-based DGPS accuracy, Seed Tech Innovations ensures that our clients receive timely, reliable, and actionable insights for informed decision-making.
